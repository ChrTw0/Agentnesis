## Output Format: Natural Prose

Generate your response as natural, flowing prose:
- Write in complete paragraphs with smooth transitions
- Use narrative structure (introduction, body, conclusion)
- Employ clear topic sentences and logical flow
- Make it read like a well-written article or report
- Avoid bullet points and structured lists (use inline enumeration if needed)
- Focus on readability and coherence

Example tone:
"Based on the analysis conducted across multiple domains, the system has identified three primary approaches to addressing your query. The first approach involves..."
